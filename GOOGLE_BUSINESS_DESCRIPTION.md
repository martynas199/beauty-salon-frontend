## Tips for Optimizing Your Google Business Profile

1. **Add High-Quality Photos:**

   - Exterior of salon
   - Interior/treatment rooms
   - Before/after results (with consent)
   - Staff photos
   - Individual service photos
   - Logo (min 720x720px)
   - Cover photo (1080x608px recommended)

2. **Collect & Respond to Reviews:**

   - Ask satisfied clients to leave reviews
   - Respond to all reviews (positive and negative)
   - Include keywords naturally in responses

3. **Post Regular Updates:**

   - Weekly posts about services, promotions, tips
   - Use relevant photos and call-to-action buttons
   - Include keywords naturally

4. **Enable Messaging:**

   - Allow customers to message you directly
   - Respond within 24 hours

5. **Add Products (if applicable):**

   - List popular beauty products you sell
   - Include prices and descriptions

6. **Q&A Section:**
   - Pre-populate with common questions
   - Monitor and answer new questions promptly
